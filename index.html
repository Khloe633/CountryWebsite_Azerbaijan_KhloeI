ABOUT

Azerbaijan is a transcontinental (crosses the farther side of the continent) country located on the boundary line between Eastern Europe and Western Asia. It is known for its various cities, buildings and customs. The country is also known as the “Land Of Fire”, (that’s what the word “Azerbaijan” literally means!). The country shares almost the same types of dishes/cultures with Turkey, so a lot of content here may also be related to Turkey.
As you explore this website, you will learn about Azerbaijan’s many cool features, including its culture, its variety of tasty foods and its unique architectural landmarks.
